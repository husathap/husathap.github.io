Projects

Current Project

Mission-Relevation Information Management for Integrated Response (MIMIR)

Jan 2018 - Now

MIMIR is a groups of projects undertaken at Dalhousie University to explore next-generation interfaces that help user to better explore maritime datasets. The projects include the use of cutting-edges technologies such as cross reality, tabletop display, and etc. My current involvement in MIMIR involves the development of a visual language to aid data query using direct manipulation, creating a virtual reality navigation aid system with the cue embedded in the peripheral vision, and using statistics to aid visualization and selection of data.

Project Website: https://gem.cs.dal.ca/projects/mimir/

Past Projects

EDUCAUSE LSRS Evaluation of Dalhousie University

May 2018 - November 2018

In order to improve the quality of classrooms at Dalhousie University, I worked with the Centre of Teaching and Learning to survey the classrooms on Dalhousie using EDUCAUSE LSRS scale to assess the suitability of the classrooms for pedagogical purposes. I performed statistical analysis on the data and provided recommendation to the university.

September 2014 - August 2015
