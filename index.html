I am currently a PhD student at Dalhousie University under the supervision of Prof. Derek Reilly. I am specialized in the area of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My current particular areas of interest are: virtual/augmented reality, visualization, and direct manipulation. Additionally, I also have interests in statistics - particularly in nonparametric methods. Previously, I was a MSc student at the University of Calgary, and I worked under the supervision of Prof. Wesley Willett. We created Kalgan, a video player designed for language learning. I was an undergraduate students at the University of Toronto with a BSc in Computer Science, Cognitive Science, and French.
