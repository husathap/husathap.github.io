Dr. Sathaporn "Hubert" Hu / สถาพร ฮู / 胡秀楷
Part-Time Faculty at School of Computer Science & Technology, Algoma University
Dr. Hu has completed his Ph.D program in Computer Science at Dalhousie University. His Ph.D. thesis is about using mixed reality technologies to better understand statistical information. He is currently a part-time faculty member at Algoma University (Sault Ste. Marie). He has taught classes in research methodology, human-computer interaction, artificial intelligence, software for sciences, and data structure.
