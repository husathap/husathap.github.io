Prof. Sathaporn "Hubert" Hu, Ph.D.

A.k.a. สถาพร ฮู [TH], 胡秀楷 [CH]

BIO: Dr. Hu is an assistant professor in extended reality at Algoma University based in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. He was a Ph.D. student at Dalhousie University under the supervision of Prof. Derek Reilly. He received his M.Sc. degree from the University of Calgary, and obtained his H.B.Sc. from the University of Toronto. His area of focus is on immersive analytics - i.e., using mixed reality technologies to visualize and enhance understanding of data and statistical information. He is also keenly interested in applying AI to extended reality.
