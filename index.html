About Me

A prototype of Gander, my current PhD project. An outdated screenshot is provided here since the project is patent pending and it is still being evaluated.

Hubert is a PhD candidate at Dalhousie University under the supervision of Prof. Derek Reilly. Currently, he is based out of Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. He currently studies computer science and is specializing in the area of human-computer interaction, augmented reality, and immersive analytics. He is now keen to get started on a virtual reality project that will help Sault Ste. Marie to become a better place.

Currently, Hubert is working on Gander. Gander is a multimodal interface that combines Augmented Reality together with a tablet interface. The goal of the interface is to help the user become better at forming linear regression models.
