Dr. Sathaporn "Hubert" Hu / สถาพร ฮู / 胡秀楷

Part-Time Professor at Algoma University

Dr. Hu is currently a part-time professor at Algoma University. He received his Ph.D. degree from Dalhousie University in 2024. His Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. Derek Reilly. He specialized in mixed reality, statistical analysis, and immersive analytics. He also had knoweldge in artificial intelligence, and cognitive science. He obtained his H.B.Sc from the University of Toronto in 2015, and his M.Sc. from the University of Calgary in 2017.
