About Me

I am a PhD candidate (Defended on January 9th, 2024). My speciality is applying human-computer interaction and immersive technologies to data exploration and statistical analyses. I also have background in cognitive science. Currently, I am a member of GEMLab, Dalhousie University. Previously, I was a MSc student at iLab, University of Calgary and a HSBc student at the University of Toronto.

Currently, I am transitioning out of my PhD program and a part-time faculty member at Algoma University. A new page will be set up once I have a permanent or a long-term position.
