Prof. Hu is an assistant professor in extended reality (XR) at Algoma University. His area of research involves using XR technologies to visualize data, and using AI to better data being generated within XR. Currently, he is looking at the expand his research area. He obtained his PhD from Dalhousie University under supervision of Prof. Derek Reilly in the area of immersive analytics. He was a MSc from the University of Calgary under the supervision of Prof. Wesley Willett. His area of specialization was computer-assisted langauge learning. He was an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, St. George.

Prof. Hu has experience teaching courses in human-computer interaction, research methodology, data science, artificial intelligence, and lower-year data structure courses. He is not teaching this semester.
