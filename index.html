About Hubert

Hubert (Sathaporn) Hu is a Master's degree student at University of Calgary. He is working in iLab and he is supervised by Professor Wesley Willett. His current Master's thesis involve making language learning simpler for everyone and visualizing linguistics model such as bigram models and part-of-speech distribution. Huber is previously a student at University of Toronto and he has a BSc. which includes a specialist in computer science, a major in cognitive science and a minor in French.
