Prof. Sathaporn "Hubert" Hu (A.k.a. สถาพร ฮู, 胡秀楷)

Assistant Professor in Extended Reality, Algoma University (Sault Ste. Marie), Faculty of Computer Science & Technology

Currently, I am not looking for research-based Master's degree student due to funding constraints. Algoma University does not allow research-based student to self-fund. Still, if the student is interested in collaborating with me, please visit Lab and Propspective Students page.

I am an assistant professor in Extended Reality (XR) at Algoma University currently afflicated with Immxrsive. I specialize in immersive analytics - i.e., using XR technologies to make data easier to understand. I received my Ph.D. degree from Dalhousie University. As a Ph.D. student, I was supervised by Prof. Derek Reilly and received funding from Ericsson through Mitacs Accelerates. For my MSc. program at the University of Calgary, I was supervised by Prof. Wesley Willett. I obtained my H.B.Sc. from the University of Toronto, which was Specialist in Computer Science, Major in Cognitive Science (Computational Stream), and French as a Second Language Minor.


As an instructor, I primarily specializes in teaching human-computer interaction courses and research methodology classes for graduate students. In the past, I taught courses in data structure, and artificial intelligence (AI).
